.DesignPattern:Problem Solving technique during building phase of software
1)Structural Design pattern:Made of Variables and Functions
2)Creational Design Pattern:Used for Creating Objects and instances within an object
3)Behavioural Design Pattern:Used for Interaction of One Class within the other
4)MVC Design Pattern
5)Depedency Injection
NOtes:All this helps in building good structured and Proffesional Interfaced Softwares

--------------------Terminology------------------------------
Object :Instance of a class
Singleton:Class having one object at a time(Limits creation of objects to 1)
Bridge DesignPattern:This DesignPAttern separates Abstraction from Implementation
Facade:Appearance of a software
